How to play

  1. On your turn, click the deck (or the discard pile) to draw a card.
  2. Green Way cards go on YOUR board. Click a glowing slot to place it.
  3. Wasteful cards can be used to block an empty slot on your opponent's board — they'll have to clear it before they can win!
  4. If a wasteful card is blocking one of YOUR slots, click it at the start of your turn to clear it (this uses your turn, no draw).
  5. Stage 1 (Device) and Stage 2 (Action) must be a matching pair (e.g. Laptop + Reuse).
  6. First to collect all 8 Green Way cards — with matching device + action — shouts "GREEN!" and wins! 🌍
